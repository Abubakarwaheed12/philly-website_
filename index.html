ABOUT ME

Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist,
Entrepreneur, Venture Studio Partner, Dog
Mom

I am a clinically trained and licensed Marriage and Family Therapist, which sounds like a
mouthful, but hear me out. As a trained LMFT, I have been trained to think in systems theory.
The idea is that instead of focusing on what’s wrong with an individual, we should actually
focus on what’s going with the system around an individual. The work I have done with my
clients over the last 7 years while I built several businesses with my partner has brought me
to a place where I am wanting to help others in a different way.

As an entrepreneur who is married to an entrepreneur, our lives are surrounded by
businesses. I have worked hard on figuring out how I can run 11 companies, be a daughter,
friend, partner, and still do the things I love. Most nights, I am tucked in my cozy bed by 10
pm and most mornings I am up by 5:30 am ready to start my day. Of course, it is not
perfect…but it’s working.

How?

I have spent the last two years focusing solely on finding balance and I want to share my
secrets with you. It is not easy and it requires A LOT of work, but after you work through all
of your systemic changes, the end result is incredible.

You can have your life and your company too. Your relationships can be solid. Let's figure
out what balance means in your life and start shifting your patterns to get you to where
you are able to find the joy, hope, and excitement again.
